• Click on the "+" to the left the name of a closed folder to view subfolders.
  • Click on the edit button to the right of a folder name to edit flypaper text.
  • Click here for help with regular expressions, but do not enclose your flypaper expressions in /slashes/.
  • Simple practical example: flypaper|muscipula|nepenthaceae will match any of those three words.
  • These will be applied without case sensitivity. Capitalization is irrelevant.
